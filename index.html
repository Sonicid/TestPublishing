What is Library Music?

Library music, also known as ‘production music’, is created mainly for Broadcast professionals producing Films, TV programmes, movie promo trailers and commercial TV and Radio advertising. It’s not written to specific visuals but, as Genre based album concepts, and distributed around the world. You can grow substantial royalty income streams from your tracks.
